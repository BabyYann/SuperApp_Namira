description About Event

The Annual Science Fair 2024 is Namira’s premier showcase of innovation and academic excellence. This year, we focus on "Sustainable Future: AI & Ecology." Join over 50 student teams as they present groundbreaking research and functional prototypes designed to solve real-world environmental challenges. The event includes keynote speeches, interactive workshops, and a prize ceremony for top-tier projects.
